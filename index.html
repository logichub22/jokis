Welcome to  Century Green Consortium

Century Green is a consortium of agribusiness entrepreneurs, corporations, and major financiers, organized for the purpose of investing in food value addition and sufficiency by managing, developing, and transforming farmlands into world class, ecofriendly assets and using best-in-class technologies, modern machinery, tools, and technical know-how to add value.  

Our Services

Our services is to produce high-quality products ranging from farm produce, fish, livestock, animal feed and consumer foods, applying renewable energy and food preservation systems, we achieve positive contributions to gainful enterprise, improved quality of life, and sustainability in agricultural land use and community prosperity.

 COLLABORATION WITH LOCAL AUTHORITIES

We work hand in hand with local municipalities to provide numerous jobs for food cultivation, processing and packaging operations, distribution, and maintenance of stakeholder farms and processing facilities.

 COLLABORATION WITH ACADEMIA

In collaboration with local university staff and our network of agriculture and nutrition experts, we research and develop local talent and technology to achieve our goal of establishing truly sustainable systems that produce highest quality foods on a commercial scale, while recapturing, repurposing, and reusing most (if not all) of our waste products. 

 COLLABORATION WITH NON-PROFIT PARTNERS

Support Social Enterprise - Non-profit, 501©3  1. Educational Training  2. Science and Innovation  3. Specialty Skills Training  4. Charitable Funding Support  5. Charitable Event Hosting  6. Research; in related fields of Engineering, Fisheries, Energy, Medicine & Biology.

RESEARCH AND REINVESTMENT

The byproduct of the research would be industrial amounts of food which could be sold for profit, reinvested for the expansion of the facility, and a percentage of the profits would be donated to local charities on a consistent basis.
